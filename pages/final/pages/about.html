We are international volunteers working in A4, Contemporary Space for Culture in Bratislava as part of the European Solidarity Corps programme. This website is here to document the various activities we are involved in inside and out of A4.
We started R0, a monthly zine conceived as a space of expression, spilling out thoughts and feelings while staying far away from home. Following this initiative, we wanted to expand our zero-space and share more of our lives, projects, as well as promoting activities we believe in.
